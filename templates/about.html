IRD-GenDB

Inherited Retinal Disease Genomic Database

Inhertied retinal diseases (IRDs) are a heterogeneous group of disorders characterized by progressive photoreceptor degeneration resulting in visual impairment or blindness. There have been over 270 genes identified to cause IRDs.

This project aims to develop a database holding clinical data surrounding IRDs, including information on many variants, such as, the gene affected, the type of mutation, the pathogenicity, and other variables. This database also contains patient records from National Taiwan University Hospital located in Taipei, Taiwan. Patient names and other sensitive information were not recorded to this database.

Chen et al. (npj Genomic Medicine, 2021)

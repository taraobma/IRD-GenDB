About Inherited Retinal Dystrophies (IRDs)

Inherited Retinal Dystrophies are a group of rare genetic disorders causing progressive vision loss due to degeneration of photoreceptor cells or the retinal pigment epithelium. They affect ~1 in 2,000 people worldwide and are caused by mutations in over 270 genes. This database catalogues patients and variants across the major IRD subtypes.
